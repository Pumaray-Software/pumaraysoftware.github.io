Process

  1. Based on your requirement we will create a rapid prototype.
  2. This prototype will be presented and demonstrated to you
  3. Based on the result of this presentation and your response the real application will be developed
  4. The application will be tested by test analyzers and company users
  5. A user manual and development specification will be documented
  6. If you want Pumaray to maintain the application a new maintenance agreement will be specified.
